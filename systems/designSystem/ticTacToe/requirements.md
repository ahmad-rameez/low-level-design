## Tic Tac Toe

### Requirements
1. It's a simple two player game, turn wise
2. Each player selects a symbol before the game.
3. Player Takes turns placing it on a 3 X 3 grid.
4. A player wins by placing three of its pieces in a row, column or diagonal.
5. The game ends when a player wins or when all grid cells are filled, resulting in a draw.


## Core Entities
1. PlayingPiece
2. Player
3. Board
4. GameEngine
